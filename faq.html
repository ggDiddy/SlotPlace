Bonuses & Promotions

Wagering requirements (also called playthrough or rollover requirements) indicate how many times you must wager your bonus before you can withdraw any winnings.

Example: If you receive a $100 bonus with 30x wagering requirements, you must wager $3,000 ($100 x 30) before you can cash out.

Important points:

  • Lower wagering requirements are better for players (20x-30x is reasonable)
  • Some bonuses require wagering on bonus + deposit amount
  • Different games contribute differently (slots usually 100%, table games 10-20%)
  • Wagering requirements typically have a time limit (7-30 days)
  • Always read the bonus terms before claiming

Responsible Gambling

Responsible gambling means enjoying gambling as entertainment while maintaining control over your time and money.

Key Principles:

  • Gamble only with money you can afford to lose
  • Set limits on deposits, losses, and playing time
  • Never chase losses
  • Don't gamble when upset, depressed, or under the influence
  • Take regular breaks
  • Balance gambling with other activities
  • Don't use gambling as a source of income

Responsible Gambling Tools:

  • Deposit Limits - Set daily, weekly, or monthly limits
  • Loss Limits - Cap how much you can lose
  • Session Time Limits - Limit playing time
  • Reality Checks - Regular reminders of time spent
  • Self-Exclusion - Block access for 6 months to lifetime
  • Take a Break - Temporary exclusion (24 hours to 6 weeks)

Warning Signs of Problem Gambling:

  • Spending more than you can afford
  • Gambling to win back losses
  • Borrowing money to gamble
  • Neglecting work, family, or personal responsibilities
  • Lying about gambling habits
  • Feeling anxious or irritable when not gambling

If you or someone you know has a gambling problem, help is available:

UK Resources:

  • GamCare - www.gamcare.org.uk | Helpline: 0808 8020 133
  • BeGambleAware - www.begambleaware.org | Helpline: 0808 8020 133
  • GamStop - www.gamstop.co.uk | Free self-exclusion scheme

International Resources:

  • Gamblers Anonymous - www.gamblersanonymous.org
  • Gambling Therapy - www.gamblingtherapy.org
  • National Council on Problem Gambling (US) - 1-800-522-4700

What These Organizations Offer:

  • 24/7 helplines and live chat support
  • Free counseling services
  • Self-assessment tools
  • Support groups and forums
  • Financial advice and debt management
  • Family support services

Remember: Seeking help is a sign of strength, not weakness. These services are confidential and free.
